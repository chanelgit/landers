# Google Search RSA — Credit Builder Offer
**Traffic Source:** Google Search | **Lander:** Quiz Funnel | **Avatar:** Struggling Sam/Samantha

---

## ⚠️ Pre-Launch Compliance Notes

Before pushing these live, sanity check against Google Ads Financial Services policy:

- **No guaranteed outcomes**: Avoid "guaranteed 100 point jump" — Google will disapprove and can flag the account. Use "designed to help," "may help," "see your potential."
- **No misleading urgency**: "Last chance today" without a real deadline = policy strike.
- **No "free" if there's a cost**: If the offer has a subscription/trial-to-paid, don't headline "100% Free." Use "Free to Start" or "No Cost to See Your Plan."
- **Trademark check**: Do not bid on or use competitor brand names in copy.
- **Landing page match**: Quiz funnel headline should echo the ad promise (score improvement, personalized plan) or Quality Score tanks.

## Why This Copy Works for Google Search + Quiz Funnel

- **Search intent match**: Google searchers are further down-funnel than social. Headlines lead with the *outcome they typed in* ("build credit faster," "fix a low score"), not curiosity teases that work on Facebook/TikTok.
- **Quiz funnel handoff**: Multiple headlines and CTAs explicitly reference the quiz ("60-second quiz," "answer 6 questions," "personalized plan") so the click-to-lander transition doesn't feel like a bait-and-switch. Quality Score benefits.
- **Avatar language**: "Denied again," "580 score," "sub-620," "skip the $99/mo repair firms" — pulled directly from Sam/Samantha's pain points and failed prior attempts, without being hype-y.
- **Compliance-safe**: No specific point-jump claims, no "guaranteed," no fake urgency. Uses "designed to," "may," and process-focused language.
- **Objection handling in-ad**: "No hard credit check" and "no cost to start" preemptively kill the two biggest click hesitations for this avatar (fear of another score drop, fear of another $99 scam).

## Testing Plan (Week 1)

1. Launch this RSA with all 15 headlines / 4 descriptions unpinned first (except CTA position) to let Google's asset optimizer find the winner.
2. After ~500 impressions per asset, prune "Low" performers and replace.
3. Build **2 more RSAs** for adjacent themes: *credit builder loan* (feature-led) and *raise score for apartment/car* (event-triggered urgency). Same avatar, different search intent.
4. Watch **quiz start rate** as the north-star metric — CTR is vanity if people bounce off the lander.
