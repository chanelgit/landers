## ⚠️ Compliance Note Before Copy

This is a financial offer subject to **Google Ads Financial Services policy**. Before running:
- No guaranteed score increases ("Raise your score by 53 points" — cannot be used as-is in Google ads; it's fine for Meta/TikTok but Google will likely disapprove specific point-increase guarantees)
- Avoid "no credit check" as a headline claim unless offer explicitly supports it in T&Cs
- Avoid implying affiliation with credit bureaus or government programs
- "Free" claims must match landing page reality (quiz funnels often gate the real offer post-quiz — make sure disclosure is clear)
- Include required financial disclosures per network policy in your final URL / lander, not just the ad

I've written headlines to stay inside typical Google financial-services guardrails while still being compelling. Flag anything with "[VERIFY]" for legal/compliance review against your actual offer terms before launch.
